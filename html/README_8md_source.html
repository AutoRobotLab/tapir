3 Toolkit for approximating and Adapting POMDP solution In Realtime (TAPIR) {#abt}
4 =========================================================================
5 
6 TAPIR is a C++ implementation of the Adaptive Belief Tree (ABT) algorithm [1].
7 ABT is an online POMDP solver capable of adapting to modifications to the POMDP
8 model without the need to reconstruct the policy from scratch.
9 
10 [1] H. Kurniawati and V. Yadav. An Online POMDP Solver for Uncertainty Planning
11 in Dynamic Environment. Proc. Int. Symp. on Robotics Research. 2013.
12 
13 For bug reports and suggestions, please email rdl.algorithm@itee.uq.edu.au
14 
15 For information on updates, please visit http://robotics.itee.uq.edu.au/~tapir
